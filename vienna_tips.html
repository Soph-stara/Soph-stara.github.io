Best Cafes in Vienna

My favourite places in Vienna which I want to share with you.

Filter by:

Innere Stadt (1st District)

Café Prückel

Stubenring 24, 1010 Wien

What to try: Their classic Wiener Melange and Sachertorte

Best times to visit: Weekday mornings to avoid tourists

A historic cafe with stunning architecture. Was once frequented by Freud, Trotsky, and other intellectuals. Slightly touristy but worth the visit for the ambiance.

Yppenplatz (16th District)

Cafe An-Do

Yppenplatz 5, 1160 Wien

What to try: Their homemade lemonade and breakfast bowl

Best times to visit: Weekend mornings for brunch

Cozy space with a lovely summer garden. Great for people watching and enjoying the vibrant atmosphere of Yppenplatz.

Cafe Frida

Yppenplatz 7, 1160 Wien

What to try: Mexican-inspired brunch and their coffee

Best times to visit: Sunny afternoons for outdoor seating

Vibrant atmosphere with delicious food. The outdoor seating area is perfect when the weather is nice.
